Top 10 Eco-Friendly Ski Resorts

As climate awareness grows, many ski resorts are transforming the winter holiday experience by prioritising sustainability. These eco-friendly destinations combine world-class slopes with innovative environmental practices—so you can enjoy the mountains while helping protect them. Here are ten of the best eco-conscious ski resorts in the world.
1. Zermatt, Switzerland A car-free village powered largely by hydroelectricity. Zermatt focuses on renewable energy, electric taxis, and sustainable mountain restaurants.
2. Avoriaz, France Built entirely car-free, Avoriaz prioritises green architecture and uses horse-drawn sleighs for transport. It’s part of the “Flocon Vert” (Green Snowflake) certified resorts.
3. Laax, Switzerland A leader in sustainability, Laax aims to become the world’s first self-sufficient alpine destination. The resort invests in solar power, zero-waste initiatives, and biodiversity protection.
4. Aspen Snowmass, USA Aspen is known for its bold climate action. The resort uses methane-to-energy systems, supports environmental advocacy, and invests in renewable energy solutions.


5. Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis, Austria This resort uses a low-emission underground train, promotes eco-mobility, and focuses on energy-efficient snowmaking and waste reduction.
6. Whistler Blackcomb, Canada A pioneer in sustainability, Whistler generates hydropower, focuses on zero waste, and restores local ecosystems through its “Fitzsimmons Creek” project.
7. Arapahoe Basin, USA A-Basin is dedicated to carbon reduction, watershed restoration, habitat protection, and sustainable food systems across its mountain lodges.
8. Flims Laax Falera, Switzerland Another Swiss leader, this area specialises in circular economy practices, green building standards, and sustainable transport.
9. Villars, Switzerland Awarded the “Flocon Vert,” Villars focuses on renewable energy, eco-mobility, and environmentally responsible ski infrastructure.
10. Saas-Fee, Switzerland A glacier village with no cars and nearly 100% renewable energy. Saas-Fee uses hydropower, solar energy, and environmentally friendly transport.
Whether you’re a devoted skier or a snowy-mountains daydreamer, these resorts prove that unforgettable winter experiences can be both exciting and environmentally responsible.

Antonio Mariano
